    <p>Erika Dietrick, known as @erika_thedev online, is a software engineer and technical content creator residing in North Carolina, USA. Partially while earning her Master of Software Engineering from East Carolina University (and later, her CCNA and CCNA Automation), she has accumulated 7 years of diverse experiences in tech, from Fortune 100s to startups alike, and across networking, cybersecurity, and traditional software engineering.</p>

    <p>During her time at Cisco (which has comprised the majority of her tech career), Erika spent time as a network support engineer, a sales engineer, and a developer advocate. These experiences gave her a unique insight into the challenges network engineers faced, particularly with automation and AI.</p>

    <p>As a Cisco developer advocate, she built a community and conversation around coding under her persona Erika the Dev, a platform meant to make coding fun, approachable, and most of all, actually relevant and practical. She traveled the globe teaching AI and coding concepts at Cisco Live, earning Distinguished Speaker in all three global regions. Her LinkedIn posts about coding, coding education, and engineering frequently amassed 1 million impressions, an experience which helped catapult the online business she runs today.</p>
